Any Old Diamonds

Lilywhite Boys #1

  • KJ Charles

Published 2019 by KJC Books

  • fiction
  • romance

As KJC says: In which we stick it to a duke.

What Band Sinister was to 2018, Any Old Diamonds is to 2019. It’s SO good. An instant favorite. I might have to re-read both to decide which one I like best, and if it’s too close to call, which only means I should read them a third and fourth time, I won’t mind a bit.

(It could be recency bias, but I do think Diamonds wins it. I mean, jewel thieves in love. Come on.)

“It’s like I was living in a pencil drawing, and you turned the world to oils.”
